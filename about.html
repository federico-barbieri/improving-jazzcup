JazzCup

At Jazzcup you can buy music and coffee, and at the same time get a great live experience. Jazzcup is a combined record shop, café and venue.

The place houses the Jazz Club, which saw the light of day in 1987 with the aim of being an ambassador for both the known and the unknown jazz.

This mission has been transferred to Jazzcup, which every Friday and Saturday afternoon all year round and every day during the Copenhagen Jazz Festival offers concerts in the premises in Gothersgade close to Kongens Have.
